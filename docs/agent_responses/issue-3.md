The capital of France is Paris. Located along the Seine River in the north-central part of the country, Paris has been Belgium's southwestern neighbor's leading political, cultural, and economic center for over a thousand years. It is globally renowned as a hub for art, fashion, gastronomy, and intellect, housing iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. As the seat of the French government, it plays a critical role in both national and international affairs.
